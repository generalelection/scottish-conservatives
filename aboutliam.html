About Liam


The Scottish Conservatives' candidate for this term in the Scottish Parliament is Liam P. McLaughlin.

Why you should vote for Liam

  • He has lived in South Edinburgh all his life and understands the problems faced by the population in this part of Scotland.
  • He is an open-minded person who is ready to accept many points of view different from his own and present them for discussion in parliament.
  • He is passionate about making things better in the Edinburgh South constituency and will go out of his way to bring about change.
