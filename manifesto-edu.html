Education Manifesto/Policy


Education is one of the most important public services needed by people. Not only is it a basic human right, all improvements to our country depend upon it. We believe that improving our education system as much as we can is the way forward, as children are the future, and the only way the future can change is if our children know not to make the same mistakes we did and to make the world a better place, and this is all achievable through a good education system.
