Justice Manifesto/Policy


We believe that crime prevention and justice are key to a successful economy and country, as citizens can go about their daily activities without fear. We are going to:

  • Introduce prison sentences of a maximum of three months as an alternative to community service.
  • Make community sentences tougher, with offenders carrying out jobs like litter picking, snow shovelling, and beach cleaning.
  • Pilot a community court in Glasgow.
  • Use cash seized from criminals to help victims of crime.
  • End automatic early release from prison.
  • Introduce mandatory drug tests for all inmates on arrival to and departure from prison.
  • Oblige police services to release monthly local crime figures and maps.

Furthermore, we also have plans against terrorism and extremism, such as:

  • New banning orders for extremist groups.
  • Introducing extremism disruption orders for those who "spread poison".
  • Restoring police and agencies' access to communications data they need to fight terrorism and organised crime.

With these steps, we believe that we can bring crime and terrorism/extremism under control.
